Protein...but not as we know it

Insect flour is naturally high in protein. For example cricket flour is higher in protein that chicken and fish. On top of that it has other important nutrients like Omega 3 and 6. It's not actually flour, but made from ground specially farmed insects bred for human consumption.Ok, healthy, but does it taste any good? »

We make insects taste great

We use cricket, locust and earth worm flours and combine them with a range of natural ingredients like nuts, dried fruits and seeds. These are then baked into health snack foods that are high protein, low carb and taste great. Insect flour has a naturally quite a nutty taste. Remind me, why else do I need to eat insects? »

Feel good about your planet

The healthy protein produced by insect farming has a significantly lower environmental impact than other meats like beef and chicken. In fact it's 9 times more efficient than beef and has a lower carbon fooprint. It's great for food security, oh, and did you know you already eat insects everyday anyway?
